System integration

We help you find the ideal system integration. We understand your supply chain needs, figure out where things need to be automated, and advise you how to run the best possible system, with minimum disruption, at minimum cost. And we support you throughout the process: available on phone or email to assist with any queries. We set up the processes and then empower you to run it yourself.

Automatically sync inventory

We integrate your applications: between Sage, SYSPRO, iSync or SAP, and Shopify, Magento, WooCommerce and B2B trade stores. And then we sync your inventory data – multiple price lists, descriptions, warehousing information and more – near real time. This means a far more efficient system, far less risk of human error, and far more time to spend doing what you do best: growing your business.

Order management

We let you receive orders from multiple sales channels and push orders to your ERP or accounting system in real time. We’ve got your orders sorted from your customer’s first click to delivery: with custom workflows suited to your ordering needs, and the option to set up specific customer accounts or integrate with fulfillment services for picking, packing and delivery. We can also automate drop shipping for you.
