It’s business as usual, just better

Want to open up new online sales channels without changing how you currently work? No problem. We’ll give your existing systems an extra boost. You carry on using your ERP or accounting system, we’ll do the heavy lifting.

Take your existing Sage, SYSPRO, iSync or SAP system online by integrating into Shopify, Magento, WooCommerce and B2B trade stores. Simply does it.

We’ll sync your online orders to your existing ERP or accounting system and automatically update your inventory levels, so you’ll never sell something you can’t deliver. Happy customers guaranteed.

Do less. Sell more

With Stock2Shop you automate tasks so you can sell as efficiently as possible. No more manual order entries or piles of paper – just clean, reliable information about what’s where. Instantly. Online orders are automatically added to your ERP or accounting system, so there’s no need for double-capture, and less chance of human error.

Sell everywhere. Painlessly

Stock2Shop keeps all your channels synchronised. The stock levels in your physical shops, online shops, B2B trade stores and marketplaces are all aligned because they’re all managed from the same single source: your ERP or accounting system.

B2B Trade store

Whether you sell to businesses, end-consumers or both, our B2B trade store makes admin easy.

Some of our clients don’t sell to the public. They use Stock2Shop to develop sophisticated customer portals (B2B trade stores). With our customisable B2B trade stores, you can sell your products to different customers at different prices.

Let your customers or sales reps see up-to-date inventory and order directly into your ERP or accounting system, straight from their mobile. The perfect solution for wholesale and distribution.
